About Us

Agan's Bakery is a third-generation old-fashioned bakery. Since we opened our doors in 1961, we’ve dedicated ourselves to being a high-quality local bakery. We only serve the absolute best made from scratch specialty desserts and treats. You’ll taste the attention and care that we put into every one of our cakes and pastries. Celebrating 60 years of service in Cartersville, Georgia!
